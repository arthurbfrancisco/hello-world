Solitude

I find peace when I'm alone, wondering in the unknown. Solitude.

I am a self-aware , it can get lonely at times but its worth the experience.

Benefits of traveling solo

  • You do not have a schedule to follow
  • People you will meet and come across will become your life long friends
  • Eat what ever and how much you want to indulge in, no picky eaters! Cheap street are the best
  • The experience once in a lifetime, it only take once to get hooked

What I learned during my time alone

  • Never depend on others
  • You become who you are, your truest form
  • Life becomes more meaningful when you experience people in rural village are happier than most people you know
  • Getting lost was the best part of the trip!
